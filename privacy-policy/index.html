Privacy Policy for Content Studio Pro

Last Updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Content Studio Pro ("we", "us", or "the Service") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use the Content Studio Pro web application (the "Service") available at https://contentstudiopro.com/.

1. Information Content Studio Pro Collects

When you use Content Studio Pro to publish content to a connected social media account (such as TikTok), Content Studio Pro processes the following information:

  • OAuth access tokens issued by TikTok after you authorize Content Studio Pro to act on your behalf.
  • Basic profile information from TikTok (display name, avatar, open id, union id) returned by the user.info.basic scope, used only to identify the connected creator inside the Content Studio Pro dashboard.
  • Video files you upload to Content Studio Pro for publication. The bytes are forwarded to TikTok's Content Posting API and are not retained on our servers after the upload completes.
  • Captions, privacy level selections, and commercial-content disclosures you enter for each post.

2. How Content Studio Pro Uses Your Information

Content Studio Pro uses the information described above strictly to:

  • Authenticate you with TikTok via OAuth 2.0.
  • Display your connected creator profile in the Content Studio Pro dashboard so you can confirm the right account is linked.
  • Forward your video, caption, privacy choice, and disclosure flag to the TikTok Content Posting API to either publish directly (video.publish) or send the post to your TikTok inbox as a draft (video.upload).

Content Studio Pro does not use your data for advertising, profiling, training of AI models, or any purpose unrelated to publishing the content you submit.

3. Sharing of Information

Content Studio Pro does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your information is shared only in the following circumstances:

  • With TikTok, to perform the API calls you initiate (publish a video, save a draft, query your basic profile information).
  • With our hosting provider Render, which runs the Content Studio Pro backend (Frankfurt, EU).
  • When required by law, court order, or to protect the rights and safety of Content Studio Pro and its users.

4. Data Retention

Content Studio Pro keeps your OAuth access token only for the duration of your active session. Tokens are stored in your browser's session storage and are discarded when you close the tab. Video files are streamed to TikTok and are not stored persistently on Content Studio Pro's servers.

5. Your Rights

Because Content Studio Pro acts as a thin client between you and TikTok, the most reliable way to revoke access is to remove Content Studio Pro from your TikTok authorized apps list:

  • Open the TikTok mobile app -> Settings and privacy -> Security and login -> Manage apps
  • Find Content Studio Pro and tap Disconnect

You also have the right, under applicable data protection law, to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data, and to object to data processing. Contact us using the address below.

6. Security

Content Studio Pro uses HTTPS for all traffic. OAuth client secrets are stored only as environment variables on the server and are never exposed to the browser. The backend service refuses to start if the secrets are missing.

7. Children's Privacy

Content Studio Pro is not intended for users under the age of 13 (or under 16 where applicable). Content Studio Pro does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

Content Studio Pro may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Material changes will be communicated through the website.
